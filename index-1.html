About me

This is Zoey, and I LOVE board games. Be it strategy games or other type of games, they are all fun to me. Surely enough, I'd like to introduce some games here.

  • Great Western Trail:  America in the 19th century: You are a rancher and repeatedly herd your cattle from Texas to Kansas City, where you send them off by train.

  • Istanbul:  In Istanbul, you lead a group of one merchant and four assistants through 16 locations in the bazaar.
